#“Some Actually Useful Things You Can Do Tomorrow”

  • Sign-up for an Organ Donor Card

  • Install the Organ Donor App on your phone

  • Give Blood

  • Organize a Charity Event on My Charity and raise money for Cystic Fibrosis

  • Donate to GOAL

  • Donate to Medecins Sans Frontiers

  • Cancel your Sky Subscription and give that money every month to the Simon Community

  • Join a democratic political party

  • Volunteer to do Meals on Wheels

  • Teach your elderly neighbour how to use the internet so they can be a Silver Surfer

  • Figure out the difference between GMT and BST

Anyone else got ideas for how people can avoidpointlessgestures and actually do something useful?
